About

PURPOSE

As specified in Constitution and Bylaws of the Arellano Alumni Association, its purpose is to promote the welfare of Arellano University by: ASPO will provide dynamic, pro-active, and responsive programs and services that will intensify and revitalized involvement of the AU ALUMNI community in University and community undertaking and to sustain strategic industry partnership for employments opportunities of Arellano Graduates. Inspire Alumni to maintain a lifelong connection with their Alma Mater.

MISSION

To enhance the involvement of the University's alumni in supporting the vision, mission and goals of the University as it represents the interests of AU graduates worldwide. Thus, ASPO is further committed to keeping the alumni informed, involved, and invested in the alumni community and the future of Arellano University.

VISION

The Alumni Services and Placement Office (ASPO) shall be an engaging and mutually beneficial lifetime link between Arellano University and its community of alumni.
